                  Introduction to Goldmoney
                
              
            
          

        
      
      
        

          
            
              Why Goldmoney?
            
            
              
                
                  Oversight
                
                
                  Publicly listed corporation on the TSX (XAU). Regulated by the JFSC. Financial statements audited by a Big Four accounting firm (KPMG).
                
              
              
                
                  Lowest Fees
                
                
                  Goldmoney offers the least expensive way to buy, sell, store, and take delivery of physical precious metals. Significantly cheaper than ETFs and coins.
                
              
            
            
              
                
                  100% Physical Backing
                
                
                  All client assets are segregated, fully-reserved (1:1), and securely stored in insured vaults around the world.
                
              
              
                
                  Redeemable Bullion
                
                
                  Precious metals are redeemable in bullion bars at vaults and Goldmoney Branches, or in a selection of coins and bars that can be shipped to clients via Schiff Gold.
                
              
            
            
              
                
                  Exclusive Research
                
                
                  Goldmoney clients have access to the world’s leading precious metals research, which is read by the most notable investors. Our team of analysts has more than 180 years of combined experience navigating markets.
                
              
              
                
                  Banking Services
                
                
                  Goldmoney Mastercard® Prepaid card (available in plastic, 18K gold, and silver). Free gold transfers and business tools for earning in gold. Multi-currency accounts in up to nine currencies and free FX conversions.
                
              
            
            
              
                
                  Retirement and Pension Accounts
                
                
                  Goldmoney offers IRA and SIPP accounts for accumulating precious metal savings and deferring tax exposure.
                
              
              
                
                  Institutional Services
                
                
                  We offer institutional services for HNW family offices, corporations, and trusts that provide access to our pricing, execution, and custody technology through an API.
