What a VPN can do for you
A VPN allows you to browse the internet safely, anonymously and freely. Once a VPN connection is established, your online data traffic will be encrypted and rerouted through an external VPN server.


The encryption ensures nobody can intercept or read any of your data. This means nobody can see exactly what websites you visit, what you download & upload and what you stream.
The rerouting of your internet connection ensures your real IP address is hidden; other entities and websites will only register the IP address of the VPN server. All your online actions will be linked to the IP address of the VPN server instead of your own IP address. Your website visits and online actions can no longer be associated with you, and you become anonymous online. The new IP address also allows you to bypass geographic restrictions of online content.
