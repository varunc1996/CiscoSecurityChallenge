How to improve your online presence via email marketing
Building a robust online presence has become a prerequisite for succeeding in online marketplace in this digital era. Several business look down on the power of email marketing while promoting their brands online, expending their resources on Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest promotions. Of course, having thousands or even millions of followers on social media is a good thing for your business, nothing beats having email marketing because just 1000 responsive subscribers are enough to feed your business. With the online marketplace becoming fiercer by the day, the only way to stay ahead of the market competition is having loyal followers who are willing to become your ambassadors, and only email marketing can afford your that opportunity.
Top five reasons to take email marketing seriously
Now, if you are unsure about spending time and money on promoting your business via email marketing, here are five facts that will douse your doubts:
- It makes it a lot easier to convert leads to sales
- There are over 3 billion active email accounts
- Offers a better channel to effectively pitch to your clients
- Email marketing is a cost efficient compared with social media promotion
- It allows you communicate better with your followers
So, where exactly do you start? Well, here are some easy to implement strategies that will help you establish a solid presence in the online marketplace via
email marketing.
Create something of high value and entice prospects with it.
Since you can't go around putting a gun on everybody's head and asking for their email, you have to entice them to do so. A classic example of this would be creating a valuable eBook and giving it away for free, while in turn asking for their emails - everybody wins at the end of the day. Creating an eBook isn't as hard as you might think. Piecing together some helpful info from the internet and then packaging them as an eBook will do. But if writing isn't your thing, you can always get someone to do it for a little fee. Fiverr and Upwork is a great place to find freelancers who can help you with this.
Find a reputable email service provider.
While trying to building a strong presence in the online marketplace, the success of your email marketing campaign depends largely on the email service provider you choose to work with. While there are several of them out there, AWeber, Infusionsoft, GetResponse and MailChimp happen to be the prime best.
Create an optimize your landing pages.
Simply put, a landing page is a distraction free page that stirs a visitor that lands on your website into taking certain actions - subscribing with their email address in this case.
Be consistent but not pestering with your emails.
If you have a new product in the pipe, your email subscribers should be the first to know. But don't go marketing all the time. Provide them with valuable stuffs as often as possible.
Email marketing just like every other endeavor requires efforts and time. When done rightly, you would be surprised how your business will stand out in the 
online marketplace.

Actually, CVV was developed by credit card companies and banks in the wake of the rising internet fraud. Basically, CVV is a 3 or sometimes 4 digit code which helps verify that a card that has been used for a particular transaction is actually in the possession of the card owner. This code is usually embossed at the back of a card, though some card companies prefer to put theirs at the front of the card.
